What should the Analytics page emphasize?

Both options include train telemetry and MPC data — the question is which story comes first.

A

Safety Operations First

Lead with speed & alert history — what happened, when, and why the controller throttled. MPC performance is framed as: "did the train respond correctly to track conditions?" Per-segment health heatmap as supporting context.


Best for: operators, demo audiences, safety review

B

Classifier & Belief Intelligence First

Lead with per-segment belief convergence — how quickly and confidently each segment was classified over time. MPC is secondary: the speed chart shows the downstream effect of belief. Entropy timeline shows uncertainty.


Best for: ML/engineering review, academic presentation

C

Split: Telemetry + Controller in Equal Sections

Two clear sections on one page: Train Telemetry (speed, alerts, position timeline) and Controller Performance (MPC target vs. commanded speed, throttle ratio, segment health heatmap). Neither dominates.


Best for: comprehensive view, milestone demos
